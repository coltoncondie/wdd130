Overview

Purpose

The purpose of our company is to scheduel a fun, safe, and educational trip With White Water Rafting river guides. We look forward to teaching both novices experiencing rafting for the first time, to the experienced rafters looking to improve their skills.

Audience

For all of those who enjoy the wild beauty of the West Coast. Do you love Camping, Fishing, and Hiking? White Water Rafting is a great way to get out and enjoy the gifts of nature. Do you want to test yourself to something new with level 1, 2, or 3 rapids, or just wanting that challenge of 4 or 5 rapids and want to do it with someone experienced. We can scheduel a memorable day or weekend get away experience for groups or indavidual 1 on 1 excursions. Our guides with White Water Rafting, have experience with Wilderness survival, hydrodynamics (reading Water), first aid, and river rescue skills. They use these skills to give our customers a safe and memorable experience.
